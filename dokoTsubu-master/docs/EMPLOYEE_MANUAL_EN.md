# ShiftFlow Employee User Guide

## 1. What you can do in ShiftFlow

ShiftFlow lets you:

- View your schedule and the published schedule for your team
- Submit next month's preferred shifts and request schedule changes or days off
- Check your paid-leave balance and request full-day, half-day, or hourly leave
- Clock in and out and review your attendance
- Request corrections to clock records
- Review schedule, request, and approval notifications
- Change your display language and password

## 2. Sign in

1. Open the ShiftFlow URL provided by your organization.
2. Enter your registered email address and password.
3. Select **Sign in**.

After five failed attempts within five minutes, sign-in is blocked for five minutes. Stop retrying and verify your credentials.

### First-time invitation

Open the invitation link and set a password of at least 10 characters. The link is valid for 24 hours and can be used once. Ask HR to reissue it if it has expired.

### Forgotten password

1. Select **Forgot your password?** on the sign-in page.
2. Enter your registered email address.
3. Open the reset email and set a new password.

Never share your password, invitation link, or reset link.

## 3. Navigation and mobile use

On a PC, navigation appears on the left. On a phone, select the menu button at the top left to open it.

- Swipe horizontally inside wide tables.
- Do not press a submit button repeatedly.
- Do not use a public or shared device. Always sign out when finished.
- Your session ends after 30 minutes without activity; sign in again to continue.

## 4. Dashboard

The dashboard shows your current-month schedule, paid-leave balance, monthly working hours, and notification status. It also includes monthly charts for working hours, overtime, and leave taken.

If a value looks incorrect, check the selected month and confirmation status before contacting your manager.

## 5. Submit preferred shifts

1. Open **Submit request**.
2. Check the displayed target month and deadline.
3. In the monthly table, select only the dates for which you have a preference: day, night, day off, or paid leave. Unselected dates remain **No preference**.
4. Selecting paid leave opens an optional reason dialog. You may leave it blank.
5. Review the selected dates and paid-leave reasons under **Preferences to submit**. Use **Add/Edit reason** when needed.
6. Select **Submit** to send the entire month at once.
7. Confirm that the status at the top of the page is **Submitted**.

Preferred shifts for the next month are due on the 15th. Employees who have not submitted are notified on the 14th. After the deadline, use a change request.

## 6. View and change schedules

### My schedule

Open **My schedule**, select the month, and review the date, work type, and status. `CONFIRMED` means the schedule has been finalized.

### Team schedule

The team schedule shows published shifts for employees in your branch and department. Data from other organizational units is not displayed.

### Change or day-off request

1. Open **Change request**.
2. Select the date and requested work type.
3. Enter a reason and submit.
4. Check request history and notifications for approval or rejection.

For a same-day emergency request, follow your organization's approved procedure, explain that it is urgent, and contact your manager. Submission alone does not finalize the change.

## 7. Request paid leave

### Check your balance

Open **Balance** to review remaining days and the history of grants, use, cancellation, and expiration.

### Submit a request

1. Open **Leave request**.
2. Select the leave date. Submit at least one day in advance; past dates are not accepted.
3. Select full day, morning, afternoon, or hourly leave.
4. For hourly leave, enter the number of hours.
5. Enter a reason and submit.
6. Check the status in request history.

Managers, active delegates, and HR can view the reason when required for their duties. Do not include unnecessary personal or medical details.

### Cancel a request

Select **Cancel** for a request that can still be cancelled. Cancelling approved leave restores the consumed balance. Contact your manager or HR about changes after the leave date.

## 8. Clock in and out

1. Open **Time clock**.
2. Select **Clock in** when starting and **Clock out** when finishing. Press the button once.
3. Open **My attendance** and verify the recorded time.

### Location data

At clock time, ShiftFlow records the time, latitude, longitude, and acquisition status. Location is used only to review the clock record. It is not used to determine misconduct or automatically block clocking.

When your device asks for location permission, review the prompt and make your choice. A denial or acquisition failure is recorded as a status. You may clock according to your organization's approved policy.

If location cannot be acquired, check the device location setting, browser permission, and network connection. Contact your manager if you cannot change the setting.

## 9. Review attendance

Open **My attendance** to review the work date, work type, clock-in/out times, late arrival, early departure, overtime, and location acquisition status.

Request a correction before monthly attendance is finalized if a clock is missing or incorrect.

## 10. Request a clock correction

1. Open **Correction**.
2. Select the attendance record.
3. Enter the correct clock-in/out times and a reason.
4. Submit and check correction history.

The attendance record changes only after approval. If the month is finalized, ask your manager to reopen it first. The before and after values are retained in the audit history.

## 11. Notifications

Notifications include preferred-shift deadlines, schedule confirmation, and request decisions. After reviewing them, you may select **Mark all as read**.

If an email does not arrive, check your spam folder and registered email address, then contact HR. Do not submit the same request repeatedly.

## 12. Language and password

Open account settings to switch between Japanese and English. A new password must contain at least 10 characters. Leave the password field empty when you only want to change the language.

## 13. Privacy and retention

Identity, schedule, attendance, leave, location, and audit data are used according to your role for workforce operation and review. Schedule, attendance, location, leave, notification, mail-delivery, and audit records are retained for five years and then deleted.

See **Privacy and location data** on the sign-in page or at the bottom of the application for details.

## 14. Getting help

When reporting a problem, provide:

- Date and time
- Device and browser
- Page you were using
- Action you took
- Message displayed

Do not send passwords, invitation/reset links, precise location, leave reasons, or an unredacted screen containing personal data by email or chat. Redact personal data and use your organization's approved support channel.
